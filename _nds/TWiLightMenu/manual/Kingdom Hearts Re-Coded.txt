 |::.. . |tation of Awakening
-`-------'---------------------------------------------------------------------
===============================================================================

  After the opening video you take control of Sora. Move to the sparkle in the
middle of the area to spawn a shield, sword, and a staff. You must choose one
and discard another. Confirm your entry to start another sequence of
conversations. When you regain control of Sora run up the path and follow the
instructions given on how to block, dodge, and gain experience as you defeat
the Shadows.

  After the battle open the menu and choose the top option, Status Matrix. Here
you can set Chips to enter Cheats. They must be connected to the CPU, so choose
the block connected to it and insert the [LEVEL UP] chip. Sora will learn [DODGE
ROLL], the command to the right of it. You can toggle Dodge Roll on/off by
moving the switch to the left (off) or to the right (on). Close the menu and
go to the save point then approach the door and select it to enter Destiny
Islands.

 |::.. . /estiny Islands                                             [04.01.00]
-`------'----------------------------------------------------------------------
===============================================================================

  After the opening scene run over to Selphie to start a conversation. Speak
with her again and then break the Bug Blocks heading towards Wakka. After the
talk with Wakka, Sora learns about the Metal Blocks (silver and white) and Prize
Blocks (purple white a yellow star), and Rare Prize Blocks (yellow with a silver
star). Climb the Metal Blocks up to reach a Rare Prize Block containing an [HP
+2]. Hop back down and talk to Wakka again then equip the HP +2 chip. There are
three other Prize Blocks around the island, the one at the highest point is a
Rare Prize Block and nets a [BLANK CHIP].

  Talk to Wakka one last time after equipping the HP +2 chip then jump on the
nearby shack to start an event. Approach the door and select it to create a
crack in it. In the System Area the objective is to destroy all the Bug Blocks
and Heartless. After you clear the two floors you are given a chance for a
reward. You can purchase items or trade your SP (Special Points) for EXP or
Munny.

the Bug Blocks do so and talk to Selphie to obtain [DEFENSE +1]. Enter the
Secret Place near the Waterfall to start a scene and the games first boss fight.

|-----------------------------------------------------------------------------|
|  The Darkside is a three stage fight. During the first stage Darkside will  |
| thrust his arm into the ground leaving itself open for attack but it also   |
| summons a few Shadows. Kill the Shadows and damage the arm until it retracts|
| and does this attack again. The other move is one that summons several Bug  |
| and Prize Blocks, destroy them to raise your score.                         |
|                                                                             |
|  In the second stage Darkside will shoot numerous blocks from his chest at  |
| you from a distance. Dodge Roll away to avoid damage and when Darkside puts |
| his arm in the ground destroy the Blocks on his arm to stun him, leaving his|
| head wide open to be damaged.                                               |
|                                                                             |
|  In the third and final stage you will face a Purple Block surrounded by a  |
| number of other Blocks. This enemy will bounce around and spin towards you, |
| simply Dodge Roll away. Utilize the Fire commands to bust through to the    |
| and get some damage in. Eventually, the yellow blocks will seperate leaving |
| the middle open. Use this time to get your damage in, but move out once the |
| blocks slow down from spinning because they quickly collapse and can cause  |
| some damage.                                                                |

===============================================================================

  Start off by heading down the ramp and talking to the Moogle to access the
shop. Purchase at least one new command then be on your way, Quick Blitz can
be combined with Fire to create Fire Edge and Sliding Dash can be combined with
Fire to make Heat Dash so buy enough to make this combination if you don't have
1800 Munny yet.

  If you head out to the right side of town you can climb a ladder to a Metal
Block. Jump from here to the Bouncy Block to reach the roof of the Accessory
Shop. Jump over to the other side and across for a Rare Prize Block containing a
[BLANK CHIP]. If you jump back to the Accessory Shop roof and jump to the Bouncy
Block to the southern end you can reach the Rare Prize Block containing a
[THUNDER]. In the alcove to the left of the Accessory Shop is another Bouncy
Block that leads to two Prize Blocks.

  Head on over to the Second District to find numerous Bug Blocks, amidst them
are four Prize Blocks. Head down to the corner against the back wall to find
Huey in danger, defeat the Heartless to obtain a [FIRE]. More Heartless come
after a conversation, introducing a Large Body in to the mix. Go behind the
Metal Blocks where Huey was and destroy the Rare Prize Block to obtain a [BLANK
CHIP]. The objective is now to find the other ducklings and the [MYSTERY PIECES]
that they have.

  Head back the way you entered to have Sora point out the door on the bottom
area. Go through it to the Alleyway and head left, destroying the Bug Blocks.
At the southern end climb up the ladder and jump across the Metal Blocks till
you reach the Prize Block to get a lot of Munny. Drop down and take out the
other Bug Blocks then jump up to the balcony and talk to Dewey to obtain a
[THUNDER]. Hop over the balconies to the south to find another Bug Block then
head north into the right corridor.

  Destroy the Bug Blocks and climb the ladder, making your way across the Metal
Blocks to a Rare Prize Block for an [HP +2]. Head back to the Second District
and protect Dewey then a System Area will become active. Locate it near the top
of the curve along the western wall. Enter the System Area to fight a chain of
Blocks that maneuvers around like a snake. Use Thunder/Thundara spells to make
quick work of the excess Blocks then focus on the main one to complete this
System Area.

Prize Blocks. One is on the top floor where Green Blocks solidify on top of each

Block for a [CURE]. Make your way to the Third District now where a Save Point
and Moogle Shop greet you.

Heartless, the Soldiers come first followed by Red Nocturne and a Large Body.
When you defeat all of them head up the ramp and jump on the Metal Blocks to
reach a Prize Block at the top. Make a long jump east to reach a Rare Prize
Block with a [DEFENSE +1] inside. If you jump south and use a Dash command at
the right time you can slide into a balcony with a Rare Prize Block containing a
[BLANK CHIP].

  Head back to the First District to start a System Area, access it via the door
that the Hooded Man goes through. Fight your way to the end of the area and grab
any Prize Blocks along the way. Once you defeat the four enemies you need to,
return to the first area to complete the System Area.

  While defeating the Heartless, destroy the Bug Blocks and use the Movable
Block to position yourself to jump to the next layer. Rinse and repeat until you
reach the top layer with three Prize Blocks. Jump down and move to the next
floor where you can combine blocks to make breakable ones. Combine a Pair Block
and continue clockwise around the middle pillar to another. At the top hit the
Pair Block down and follow it then make it connect with the one on the bottom.
Return to the top to reach floor three.

Soldiers. Defeat them and use the Bouncy Blocks nearby to reach a higher
platform. Ride the Metal Blocks to the opposite side and get the two Prize
Blocks. Jump down and climb the ladder on the opposite end to see 4 Pair Blocks
that can be connected. Smack the first one down and run across the bridge of
Ghost Blocks, smack this Pair Block down too. Make your way back across and to
the other two, simply connect them. Jump down and connect the last two and

~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
Bug Level

  The metre in the top right corner shows the Bug Level of every room. The Bug
Level affects enemy strength, and can also influence the Bug Blocks. The color
of the meter is significant.
    Yellow     Level 1: Metal blocks and damage blocks will transform into
                        normal blocks or prize blocks.
    Red        Level 2: Damage from enemies will be multiplied by two.
    Flashing   Level 3: Damage from enemies will be multiplied by three.
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

  Destroy the first areas Bug Blocks and move to the green star. Here you are
briefed on how to battle.

~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
Battle
  When you run into Heartless, a battle begins. The battle starts as soon as you
engage the Heartless. You'll lose HP when enemy attacks make it through, so keep
an eye on your remaining HP.

Knockback*
  If you land several attacks in a row, your enemy will suffer a knockback.
Looks like attacking a knockbacked enemy will always hit!

Critical
  When you attack, press the attack button at the right time to score a critical
hit! It hits for more damage and causes a knockback more often. Sometimes, it
might even trigger a special attack.

Block
  If you press Guard at the proper time you will halve the damage done!
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

  Each world in Castle Oblivion has three different endings, either the Normal
Ending, Alternate Ending, or Extra Ending. Return to the world until you get
all three endings in each of them for a special reward!

  Head through the door and use the Destiny Islands card. Talk to Selphie and
destroy the Bug Blocks. Talk to Wakka and jump up top to the Rare Prize Block
with [HP +8]. Fight Tidus then talk to the Cloaked Man to receive the [W1 NORMAL
CARD]. Sora gains cards to the rest of the worlds, return to Destiny Islands.
In the top right corner is a Rare Prize Block with [STRENGTH +4]. Destroy the
Bug Blocks near here to reveal a green star, defeat the shadows and move to the
next star. Defeat more Shadows and after some events you gain the [W1 EXTRA
CARD]. Return to Destiny Islands once more and choose the bottom option when
talking to Selphie. Destroy all the blocks and talk to Wakka then choose the
bottom option. Tidus appears trapped in Bug Blocks, destroy them to receive the
[W1 ALTERNATE CARD].

  Head to Traverse town this time. Jump up to the northern end and break the
Rare Prize Block for a [DEFENSE +4]. At the southern end match up the Pair
Blocks and break it for a [MAGIC +3]. In the top right corner is a Heartless,
defeat the group and talk to Huey. Head to the west side and talk to Dewey and
send him back to Cid. Jump up in the middle and bust down the Bug Blocks to
reveal Louie and send him back to Cid. Head back towards Cid and defeat the
Heartless. Talk to Cid to receive the [W2 NORMAL CARD]. Return to Traverse Town
and save the ducklings in the order Huey, Louie, then Dewey to receive the [W2
ALTERNATE CARD]. Go back once more and save the ducklings in the order Louie,
Dewey, then Huey to get the [W2 EXTRA CARD].

  Next up is Wonderland. Destroy the Bug Block in the corner behind Alice to
and examine the corner to find the Cheshire Cat. The answer to his riddle is
the letter "D." Talk to Alice and tell her what her name is, either "Alice" or
"The Queen of Hearts". In the next room talk to the Heart on the left and
deliver the Potion to the Spade or alternatively give it to the other Heart.
Speak with the White Rabbit and he will take off dropping his watch, either
run in to the green star or avoid it and talk to the White Rabbit again. If
you did everything correctly, ie; solved the riddle, told Alice her real name,
made the delivery properly, and didn't touch the watch you get the [W3 ALTERNATE
CARD]. If you did everything wrong, ie; gave up on the riddle, lied to Alice,
gave the delivery to the other Heart, and picked up the watch you get the [W3
EXTRA CARD]. If you did one to three things wrong then you get the [W3 NORMAL
CARD], return to the world and get each card. Don't miss the Rare Prize Block
in a corner for the [THUNDER RESISTANCE +4].

  The next world is Olympus Coliseum. Break the Rare Prize Block in the bottom
left corner for a [THUNDER +4], the one in the top right nets [AERO +4]. Talk
with Hercules to battle a bunch of Heartless. Next, speak with Cloud and beat
the Heartless that he asks you to defeat. Then defeat the Heartless that moves
first in the second trial or you will take a 7 second time penalty. After this
round talk to Hades and beat the Heartless as fast as possible. If you get more
than 20 on Hercules' challenge, pass the challenge from Cloud, but fail to
defeat the Heartless in Hades round in under one minute you get the [W4 NORMAL
CARD]. If you fail the first two challenges you get the [W4 ALTERNATE CARD]. If
you pass all three you get the [W4 EXTRA CARD].

  Head off to Agrabah next. Iago is hidden in one of the Prize Blocks and you
have 90 seconds in which to find him. Two of the Prize Blocks are actually Rare
Prize Blocks with a [MAGIC +4] and [DEFENSE +3]. If you catch Iago with more
than one minute left on the clock you get the [W5 EXTRA CARD]. If you break all
the blocks and still catch Iago you get the [W5 ALTERNATE CARD]. If you catch
Iago with less than a minute to go you get the [W5 NORMAL CARD].

  The last world to go to is Hollow Bastion. Hit the switch in the top left
then the one in the bottom right to open the door. Do the same thing in the
next room except you have to defeat the Heartless in order to reach the second
switch. The next room is much the same, destroy the blocks that heed your path
in order to make the timer. The next room is a boss fight with Pete. The final
room is a challenge with Maleficent to reach the other side as fast as possible.
The first Rare Prize Block holds a [MAGNEGA] and the second a [SHOCK FALL]. If
you beat Pete in under 30 seconds and reach Maleficent in under one minute you
receive the [W6 EXTRA CARD]. If you take longer than the times on one but not
the other you receive the [W6 NORMAL CARD]. If you take longer than one minute
on Pete and longer than two minutes to reach Maleficent you reach the [W6
ALTERNATE CARD]. Upon reaching the door after collecting every card Leon and
Yuffie will give you a [DEBUG DEVICE: Y] and a [HEAT SINK BELT].

  After completing all the worlds you will enter a boss battle against the
figure.

/=============================================================================\
|                             Olympus Coliseum                      [06.00.00]|
\=============================================================================/
 ¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯

  If you head back to the Olympus Coliseum after clearing it and replay the
story mode you will notice you get the option to play 5 or 15 Layers. This go
around choose 15 and follow the walkthrough for the first 5 layers. After you
beat Hades a Warp Point pops up letting you progress deeper. Once you clear the
15 Layer Challenge you can choose 30 Layers once you come back. After beating
the boss on the 15th Layer you gain access to the 16th Layer.

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
LAYER 06
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

  You start off in the bottom left corner of the Layer. Destroy the Bug Blocks
and the two Heartless groups then proceed right. Here you will find three
Prize Blocks, numerous Bug Blocks, two groups of Heartless, and the Warp Point
to Layer 07. Clear everything out and continue up, but don't destroy the Bug
Blocks. Instead use them to reach the Prize Blocks high above then clear out
this room. Head left to the final room and clear it out to finish off this
Layer, head to Layer 07.

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
LAYER 07
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

  This Layer turns out to be one giant room with tons of creeping Heartless and
hardly any Bug Blocks to lower their level. Once you do lower the level to one
their will be numerous Prize Blocks available to you. Amongst them is a Rare
Prize Block containing a [BLANK CHIP]. Destroy the rest of the Blocks to reveal
the Warp Point to Layer 08.

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
LAYER 08
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

  Destroy all the Blocks in the starting area and move right to the next room.
There are three Heartless here, destroy them to drop the Bug Level down to one
and destroy the Bug Blocks. Head back to the starting area and go south to find
two more groups of Heartless. After defeating them you can clear this room and
head north where only one Heartless lurks. Clear the north room and use the
Warp Point nearby to reach Layer 09.

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
LAYER 09
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

  You start off this Layer encased in Metal Blocks so you must defeat at least
one of the Heartless groups to advance. Move onward to the next area and clear
it out the jump down going right. In this final area there are numerous Blocks
and Heartless. The Rare Prize Block contains a [MAGIC +1]; take the Warp Point
to Layer 10.

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
LAYER 10
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

  In this Layer you actually get a chance to save before progressing. You start
at the top left, clear this room and then the one to the right. Make your way
down clearing out the rooms to each side of you. The room that has the Warp
Point changes every go around so clear till you find it. You don't have to clear
every room but the bottom left room has a Rare Prize Block with a [DEFENSE +1]
inside.

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
LAYER 11
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

  You get a chance to save here as well before progressing. Destroy the Bug
Blocks in the way and move on to the next room. There are a few Heartless that
lurk about and numerous Damage Blocks at the top end. In that top end is the
Warp Point to Layer 12.

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
LAYER 12
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

  The starting room has two Heartless groups and a bunch of Damage Blocks in
the way. Clear the room and move to the next where it's close quarters with a
lot of Damage Blocks. Clear this room and move on to the last one where you will
find the Warp Point to Layer 13 and a Rare Prize Block containing a [SPARK
SWEEP].

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
LAYER 13
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

  Clear out the initial room and then choose the top room in the next area to
find the Warp Point. The bottom room holds nothing more than easy points in the
form of Bug Blocks and Heartless. Move onward to Layer 14 when you finish up.

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
LAYER 14
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

  You get an opportunity to save before being thrust in to battle in this giant
room. Clear out all the Heartless groups and the Bug Blocks, then the Rare Prize
Block to obtain a [HEAT STORM]. Use the Warp Point to move on to Layer 15.

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
LAYER 15
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

  Use the save point this time as a boss fight is coming up. Proceed through the
gate and up the steps to find Hades' new pet.

 _____________________________________________________________________________
|-----------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| BOSS: Rock Colossus                                                         |
|-----------------------------------------------------------------------------|
|  The Rock Colossus has a lot of HP and decent defense so melee attacks won't|
| do so well. Fortunately, the Rock Colossus has limited attacks but it's a   |
| little hard to time the guard. The attacks are a punch to the ground that   |
| fires waves at the entire party and a stomp. Bring high level commands like |
| Tornado and Thunder Tracer to utilize Breath of Zeus and Zeus Spear for     |
| quick, high damage rounds. A Curaga isn't a bad idea incase you are taking  |
| too long to take down the mammoth.                                          |
|-----------------------------------------------------------------------------|
|                                   PRIZES                                    |
|-----------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| Item          | Wristbands                                                  |
|-----------------------------------------------------------------------------|
 ¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
LAYER 16
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

  In order to reach Layer 16 you must first complete the round of 15 then choose
30 Layer Challenge. You start in the top right area with a lot of Bug Blocks.
Head left after clearing this room to find a Rare Prize Block containing a
[BLIZZARD SLAM]. Head south into the next room to find the Warp Point. Go right
to find the last room and earn some extra points.

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
LAYER 17
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

  You start out between two rooms. Clear the one you are in then move left to
clear the second room. The room on the far right has the Warp Point to Layer 18.

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
LAYER 18
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

  This is another giant room where you must defeat all the Heartless and Blocks
to get the rewards. The rewards is the numerous Prize Blocks that yield tons of
points. Move on to Layer 19 via the Warp Point.

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
LAYER 19
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

  You get to save and heal up before entering this Layer. You start off in the
far right room with a singular Heartless to fight. The Rare Prize Block has an
[ICE DASH] inside; clear the room and move left. Clear this room and move to the
last one with the Warp Point to Layer 20.

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
LAYER 20
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

  Clear the initial room and move left to another room. You have to defeat most
of the Heartless and Bug Blocks to drop the level down. Head through the far
left door and up to discover a Rare Prize Block with an [ICE DIVE]. In the top
left corner you will find another Rare Prize Block containing a [WIND DASH].
Go through the rooms until your find the Warp Point, it moves around each play
through.

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
LAYER 21
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

  You get a chance to heal and save again here. From this Layer forward you will
begin to fight the toughest Heartless in the game. Clear the initial room then
go either left or right and clear those rooms. Continue north where the paths
split and clear the rooms until you find the Warp Point as its location varies.

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
LAYER 22
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

  You start at the far left side of the map in this Layer. Go south as you
clear rooms until you reach an alcove that stretches left. Clear this room then
start heading north clearing more rooms with Prize Blocks and Bug Blocks. As
you go right you will find the last room. The Warp Point is hidden in one of
the rooms in this massive layer.

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
LAYER 23
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

  Once again a chance to heal and save. You start at the bottom middle so you
can go either left or right. In this layer the Warp Point can be anywhere, so
keep searching until you find it.

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
LAYER 24
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

  This ends up being one giant room where the Bug Level starts at 3! If you
haven't figured it out yet, Zeus's Spear and Breath of Zeus are strong enough
to wipe out the entire battlefield so get to using them. Lower the Bug Level to
1 and you can open the Rare Prize Block for a [MAGNERA].

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
LAYER 25
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

  Save and heal up if you need to and proceed to this layer.

 _____________________________________________________________________________
|-----------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| BOSS: Behemoth                                                              |
|-----------------------------------------------------------------------------|
|  This boss has a ton of health points, so be sure to bring Strength-L. Use  |
| some Licenses off the start like Regen-L, Strength-L, and Defense-L to gain |
| an edge. Combo your melee attacks to get the most out of your damage and    |
| continue to use attacks like Breath of Zeus and Zeus's Spear to deal the    |
| most damage. The Behemoth only has two attacks and they are both simple to  |
| guard against. One is where it reigns down lightning from the skies and the |
| other is a simple stomp that causes a shockwave.                            |
|-----------------------------------------------------------------------------|
 ¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯¯

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
LAYER 26
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

  Save and heal up after that boss fight and continue on to Layer 26. There are
four rooms and either of them can hold the Warp Point. There are however quite
a few Prize Blocks so you might want to clear them all for the points.

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
LAYER 27
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

  This Layer is filled with rooms and lots of Blocks. Section it off and start
with the bottom three rooms, then the middle three, and finally the top three.
The middle room at the top has a Rare Prize Block with [THUNDER RAID] while the
top right room has the [METAL CHOCOBO] Keybalde in it's Rare Prize Block.

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
LAYER 28
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

  Save and heal up again then continue on. This layer isn't that large so you
get a break this go around. You are forced to clear around to the left and then
down because of the Metal Blocks.

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
LAYER 29
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

  Only two rooms to this layer, the Warp Point is obviously in the second room.

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
LAYER 30
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

  Use this opportunity to save and heal up for the final battle.
 _____________________________________________________________________________
|-----------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| BOSS: Ice Colossus                                                          |
|-----------------------------------------------------------------------------|
|  The Ice Colossus is weak against fire so being able to cast Explosion will |
| be handy in this battle. The Ice Colossus will fire shards of ice that hit  |
| for a lot of damage so do your best to guard against them. Use all the      |
| Licenses that you have to boost your stats as much as possible. The other   |
| attacks that can be used against you are a breath of icicles and hale from  |
| the sky. All the attacks do a decent amount of damage so heal when needed   |
| and keep up your guarding to minimize the damage.                           |
